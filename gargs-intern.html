Gargs Engineering Limited — Manufacturing Cost Analysis Internship

SQL Python Pandas Power BI Excel Statistics Data Analysis EDA Data Visualization

Overview

Worked with manufacturing data from the backhoe bucket production process to analyze raw material consumption, production costs, and operational efficiency. The objective was to identify cost drivers and recommend data-driven improvements to optimize manufacturing performance.

What I Did

  • Collected, cleaned, and analyzed manufacturing and material cost data.
  • Used SQL to query production data and Excel for data cleaning and analysis.
  • Built Power BI dashboards to visualize material consumption, production costs, and key performance metrics.
  • Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify trends, inefficiencies, and opportunities for process improvement.
  • Collaborated with the engineering team to evaluate material utilization and production workflows.

Key Results

  • Identified major cost drivers affecting backhoe bucket manufacturing.
  • Provided data-driven recommendations to improve material utilization and production efficiency.
  • Contributed to initiatives that resulted in an 8% reduction in manufacturing costs.
  • Developed interactive dashboards that enabled stakeholders to monitor production and cost-related KPIs more effectively.
